ABOUT

Really Reel Ginger is a ginger-based beverage business in Philadelphia, Pa: My wife and I started Really Reel Ginger due to the lack of and demand for real ginger beverages. Our innovative product is in taking a value added approach of transforming the highly beneficial ginger root into a delicious beverage. My name is Iliyaas Muhammad . My wife Hadia Muhammad and I are the founders of Really Reel Ginger. Really Reel Ginger is a ginger based beverage business in Philadelphia, Pa. My wife and I started Really Reel Ginger due to the lack of and demand for real ginger beverages.   

I graduated from Pennsylvania State University with a Bachelors of Science in Psychology with a Business option. While I was in Mali, West Africa serving forty-four (44) months as a United States Peace Corps volunteer, I learned a great deal concerning the rich history of Africa, it’s people, and traditional recipes. During this period of my development, I realized that aside from ginger ale, which has little to no ginger in it, there were few real ginger beverages in the US market. I recognized that lack of pertinent knowledge and years of routine were to blame for this oversight.   

Once I returned to the US, I decided that entrepreneurship was the best avenue to take to become financially independent. After testing a number of business ventures, my wife , who was born in Mali West Africa, encouraged me to revisit the possibility of working with ginger. My wife and her family has been making ginger beverages for decades in Mali. So we developed the age old family recipe to create Really Reel Ginger.  

Really Reel Ginger target market are individuals that have a clear understanding of the benefits of ginger. This understanding  only come after proper education of ginger. For this reason, Really Reel Ginger sales team conducts presentations to make customers aware of the benefits of ginger and Really Reel Ginger's other beneficial ingredients. 

Ginger has been known throughout history to have many benefits such as being an/a: anti-emetic/anti-nausea, anti-clotting agent, antispasmodic, anti-fungal, anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, antibacterial, antiviral, antitussive, analgesic, circulatory stimulant, carminative, expectorant, increases blood flow, promotes sweating,and relaxes peripheral blood vessels.
